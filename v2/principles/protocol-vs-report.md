# Principle — Protocol vs Report

**Weight**: Recommended

## Description

Research Protocol (before) ≠ Research Report (after).

## When to Apply

- Assessment SPECs
- Investigation phases

## Rules

- Protocol: questions, not conclusions
- Report: findings, evidence, decisions
- Never mix them

## Research Protocol Contains

- Research Question
- Motivation
- Investigation Strategy
- Evidence Required
- Success Condition

## Research Protocol Does NOT Contain

- Findings
- Conclusions
- Decisions
